About Game Box

Fun • Learn • Play – Worldwide

Who We Are

Game Box is an international fun and learning platform created with love and care for children, teens, and families. Developed by Ashish Yadav (IT Engineer), our goal is to make education exciting through interactive games that encourage curiosity, logic, and joy of learning.

Our Vision

We believe that learning should never feel like a task—it should feel like play! Game Box combines creative puzzles, memory games, math practice, and color challenges designed to stimulate young minds and refresh adults alike.

Safe, Simple & Smart

Game Box does not collect personal data, login credentials, or sensitive information. All games are safe for kids and can be enjoyed by adults who want a quick mental refresh or light entertainment.
